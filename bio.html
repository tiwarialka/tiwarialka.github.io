About Me

Hi, I am Alka Tiwari exploring hydrometeorology in extreme weather situations. My research focuses on understanding extreme rainfall events and improving streamflow predictions using innovative data-driven and AI-based approaches. I love turning complex data into actionable insights for adaptation and water management. Outside the lab, I am an endurance athlete, I would rather be in the game than sit and watch it, literally and figuratively. When feasible I spend time listening and understanding art, history and interdisciplinary research.
